Delete Account – Danish Dolphins

Users of the Danish Dolphins app can request deletion of their account and associated personal data by email.

Email for deletion requests: your-email@example.com

Suggested subject: Delete my Danish Dolphins account

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to your-email@example.com.
  2. Use the subject line Delete my Danish Dolphins account.
  3. Include the email address associated with your account in the app.

What data will be deleted

After the request is verified and processed, the following data associated with the user account will be deleted from our systems where applicable:

  • account record in authentication system;
  • personal account-related data stored in the application database;
  • photos uploaded by the user;
  • sighting reports and related records associated with the user account.

How deletion is performed

Account and data deletion requests are processed manually by the developer. The requested data is removed from services used by the app, including authentication, database, and storage systems.

What data may be retained

In some cases, limited technical records, backup copies, or logs may remain temporarily until they are automatically removed in the normal system retention cycle.

If any research data has already been irreversibly anonymized and can no longer be linked to the user, it may be retained for scientific research purposes.

Retention period

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days of receiving the request.

Contact
